Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
What is Gib Flow?
Gib Flow is a mobility app designed to help residents and visitors navigate Gibraltar more efficiently. 
It provides:
    • Key wayfinder points
    • Cycle parking locations
    • Bus stops and routes
    • Secure bicycle parking booking
    • Employer-hosted parking access
    • (Future) real-time bus tracking and notifications
How do I create an account?
    1. Download the app from your app store.
    2. Open the app and select Create Account.
    3. Pay the one off fee of £1.69
    4. Enter your details and verify your email.
You must be at least 16 years old to register without parental consent. 
Do I need an account to use the app?
You can view basic navigation and transport information without an account.
However, you must create an account to:
    • Book secure bicycle parking
    • Access digital unlocking features
    • Manage employer-hosted facilities

Secure Bicycle Parking
How do I book a secure bicycle parking space?
    1. Open the app.
    2. Select a participating secure facility.
    3. Choose your date and time.
    4. Confirm and complete payment (if applicable).
You will receive confirmation in the app.
How does digital access work?
Your smartphone acts as a digital key. When you arrive:
    1. Open the booking in the app.
    2. Select Unlock Facility.
    3. Follow the on-screen instructions.
Access is valid only for the duration of your booking.
Is my bicycle guaranteed to be safe?
We implement secure access controls; however, no facility can guarantee zero risk. Users are responsible for locking their bicycles appropriately inside the facility.
What happens if I arrive late?
Your booking remains valid for the time slot reserved. If you miss your booked period entirely, refunds may not be available unless stated otherwise in the booking terms.
What if the facility does not unlock?
    • Ensure you have an active booking.
    • Confirm Bluetooth, internet, and location services are enabled.
    • Refresh the app and try again.
If the issue persists, contact support immediately.
My employer offers secure bike parking through Gib Flow — how do I access it?
Your employer will provide instructions for registration. Once registered:
    1. Log into your Gib Flow account.
    2. Navigate to My Facilities.
    3. Book and unlock as permitted under your company’s arrangement.

Who manages the facility?
The physical facility is operated by your employer or the designated site operator. Gib Flow provides the digital booking and access platform.
Can employers customise access times?
Yes. Employers may set:
    • Access hours
    • User permissions
    • Capacity limits
    • Internal booking rules
How do I pay for secure parking?
Payments can be made in-app using approved digital payment methods.
Are bookings refundable?
Refund policies depend on the facility and booking type. Details will be shown before confirmation
Can I extend my booking?
If space is available, you may extend your booking through the app before your session expires.
What items are prohibited in secure facilities?
You must not store:
    • Hazardous materials
    • Illegal items
    • Flammable substances
    • Non-mobility related storage items
Facilities are for bicycles, e-scooters, and approved mobility devices only.
What if I notice damage or suspicious activity?
Report it immediately through the app (if reporting is enabled) or contact support.
Can I share my booking or access with someone else?
No. Access is linked to your account and must not be shared. Sharing access may result in suspension of your account.

Does the app show bus routes?
Yes. Gib Flow provides bus stop locations and route information within Gibraltar.
Will the app show real-time bus tracking?
We are working towards integrating real-time tracking in partnership with local transport operators. Once available, users will be able to:
    • View live bus locations
    • Receive notifications when their bus is approaching
    • Get updates on delays
Why might bus information be inaccurate?
Bus data may rely on third-party systems. Delays, traffic conditions, or technical issues may affect accuracy.
Always allow additional time when planning important journeys.
I forgot my password — what should I do?
    1. Select Forgot Password on the login screen.
    2. Follow the reset instructions sent to your email.
How do I update my account details?
Go to Account Settings within the app to update:
    • Email address
    • Contact details
    • Payment methods
The app is not working properly. What should I do?
Try the following:
    • Restart the app
    • Check your internet connection
    • Update to the latest version
    • Restart your device
If the issue continues, contact support.
Does Gib Flow track my location?
Location data may be used to provide navigation features and facility access, but only where you grant permission in your device settings.
For full details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
