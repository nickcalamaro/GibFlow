Welcome to the GibFlow Help Centre. Here you'll find guidance on navigation features, secure bicycle parking, employer facilities, payments, and upcoming real-time bus tracking across Gibraltar.

## General

### What is GibFlow?

GibFlow is a mobility app designed to help residents and visitors navigate Gibraltar more efficiently.

It provides:

- Key wayfinder points across Gibraltar
- Cycle parking locations
- Bus stops and routes
- Secure bicycle parking booking
- Employer-hosted parking access
- Real-time bus tracking and notifications *(coming soon)*

### How do I create an account?

1. Download the app from your app store.
2. Open the app and select **Create Account**.
3. Pay the one-off fee of £1.69.
4. Enter your details and verify your email.

You must be at least 16 years old to register without parental consent.

### Do I need an account to use the app?

You can view basic navigation and transport information without an account. However, you must create an account to:

- Book secure bicycle parking
- Access digital unlocking features
- Manage employer-hosted facilities

## Secure Bicycle Parking

### How do I book a secure bicycle parking space?

1. Open the app.
2. Select a participating secure facility.
3. Choose your date and time.
4. Confirm and complete payment (if applicable).

You will receive confirmation in the app.

### How does digital access work?

Your smartphone acts as a digital key. When you arrive:

1. Open the booking in the app.
2. Select **Unlock Facility**.
3. Follow the on-screen instructions.

Access is valid only for the duration of your booking.

### Is my bicycle guaranteed to be safe?

We implement secure access controls, but no facility can guarantee zero risk. You are responsible for locking your bicycle appropriately inside the facility.

### What happens if I arrive late?

Your booking remains valid for the time slot reserved. If you miss your booked period entirely, refunds may not be available unless stated otherwise in the booking terms.

### What if the facility does not unlock?

- Ensure you have an active booking.
- Confirm Bluetooth, internet, and location services are enabled.
- Refresh the app and try again.

If the issue persists, contact support immediately at [info@gibflow.gi](mailto:info@gibflow.gi).

### What items are prohibited in secure facilities?

You must not store:

- Hazardous materials or flammable substances
- Illegal items
- Non-mobility related items

Facilities are for bicycles, e-scooters, and approved mobility devices only.

### Can I extend my booking?

If space is available, you may extend your booking through the app before your session expires.

### Can I share my booking with someone else?

No. Access is linked to your account and must not be shared. Sharing access may result in suspension of your account.

### What if I notice damage or suspicious activity?

Report it immediately through the app (if reporting is enabled) or contact our support team.

## Employer & Private Facilities

### My employer offers secure bike parking through GibFlow — how do I access it?

Your employer will provide registration instructions. Once registered:

1. Log into your GibFlow account.
2. Navigate to **My Facilities**.
3. Book and unlock as permitted under your company's arrangement.

### Who manages the facility?

The physical facility is operated by your employer or the designated site operator. GibFlow provides the digital booking and access platform.

### Can employers customise access?

Yes. Employers may configure:

- Access hours and capacity limits
- User permissions
- Internal booking rules

## Payments

### How do I pay for secure parking?

Payments can be made in-app using approved digital payment methods.

### Are bookings refundable?

Refund policies depend on the facility and booking type. Full details will be shown before you confirm a booking.

## Bus & Navigation

### Does the app show bus routes?

Yes. GibFlow provides bus stop locations and route information across Gibraltar.

### Will the app show real-time bus tracking?

We are working towards integrating real-time tracking in partnership with local transport operators. Once available, you will be able to:

- View live bus locations
- Receive notifications when your bus is approaching
- Get updates on delays

### Why might bus information be inaccurate?

Bus data may rely on third-party systems. Delays, traffic conditions, or technical issues can affect accuracy. Always allow extra time when planning important journeys.

## Account & Technical

### I forgot my password — what should I do?

1. Select **Forgot Password** on the login screen.
2. Follow the reset instructions sent to your email.

### How do I update my account details?

Go to **Account Settings** in the app to update your email address, contact details, and payment methods.

### The app is not working properly. What should I do?

Try the following steps:

1. Restart the app.
2. Check your internet connection.
3. Update to the latest version from your app store.
4. Restart your device.

If the issue continues, contact support.

### Does GibFlow track my location?

Location data may be used to provide navigation features and facility access — but only where you grant permission in your device settings. You can revoke permission at any time. For full details, see our [Privacy Policy](/privacy/).
